Basic Baseball Stats

These are the traditional stats every fan should know. They help measure a player's ability to hit and get on base. While they don't tell the full story, they're the foundation of baseball analysis.

Common Basic Stats

Stat Full Name Formula What It Measures
AVG Batting Average Hits / At-Bats How often a player gets a hit
OBP On-Base Percentage (Hits + Walks + HBP) / (AB + BB + HBP + SF) How often a player reaches base
SLG Slugging Percentage Total Bases / At-Bats Measures power-hitting ability
OPS On-base Plus Slugging OBP + SLG Overall hitting value

Other Basic Stats to Know

  • RBI: Runs Batted In
  • HR: Home Runs
  • Runs: Times a player scores
  • SB: Stolen Bases
